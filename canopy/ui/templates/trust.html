Trust Policy

  • New peers should stay pending until they have a real label, a known role, and stable behavior.
  • Safe is for explicit operational trust. Guarded is for active review, not passive clutter.
  • Restricted keeps peers off sensitive paths. Quarantine isolates them completely until you decide otherwise.
  • If a peer only shows a fallback label or no endpoints, treat it as incomplete or stale until verified.
